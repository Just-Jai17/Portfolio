Case Study 01
SMB Digital Transformation:
From Zero to Scalable Growth
How strategic Google Ads onboarding and disciplined campaign architecture accelerated a small business from minimal digital presence to measurable, scalable ROI — a methodology consistently applied across the Concentrix SMB portfolio.
↑ 35%
Conversion Rate Lift
↓ 28%

4.2×

The Challenge
The client — a local service SMB — had attempted Google Ads independently with minimal results. Budget was being spent broadly with no clear campaign structure, resulting in high CPC and near-zero lead generation. They needed a full strategic reset.
  • Wasted spend across poorly matched keywords
  • No conversion tracking in place
  • Generic ad copy with no audience alignment
  • No bid strategy tied to business outcomes
The Strategy
Led a structured onboarding session to map campaign objectives to Google Ads campaign types. Implemented keyword architecture using exact and phrase match groupings, established conversion tracking, and built audience-specific ad variations.
  • Full account audit and keyword restructure
  • Implemented Target CPA smart bidding
  • Created 3 tightly themed ad groups per service
  • Enabled Google Analytics goal tracking
Execution
Monthly optimization sessions with the client aligned budget reallocation to top-performing ad groups. Extensions were added for call, sitelink, and location to maximize search real estate and improve CTR above the industry average.
Outcome
Within 90 days, the account was generating consistent, trackable leads at a CPA 28% below the initial target. ROAS climbed to 4.2x and the client expanded budget by 40% — a direct vote of confidence in the campaign strategy.
Case Study 02
Account Expansion & Retention:
Turning Clients into Long-Term Partners
Inspired by real-world data-driven strategies, this case study illustrates how disciplined account management and consistent performance reporting converts satisfied clients into advocates who grow their investment over time.
+67%
Account Budget Growth
+24%
Conversion Increase
↓16%
Cost Reduction
The Scenario
An existing SMB client had been running a Search campaign successfully for 6 months but was hesitant to expand spend. Performance was solid but plateauing — a typical inflection point where the advisor's role becomes critical.
  • Flat month-over-month conversion volume
  • Untapped Display and Remarketing channels
  • Client skeptical about additional investment
  • Competitor activity increasing in the vertical
The Approach
Presented a data-backed expansion proposal using existing campaign performance as proof-of-concept. Recommended incremental investment into a Remarketing Display campaign targeting website visitors who hadn't converted, paired with a Performance Max trial.
  • Audience segmentation by funnel stage
  • Remarketing list creation via Google Analytics
  • Performance Max with product feed integration
  • Monthly review cadence for trust-building
Relationship Strategy
Regular performance storytelling — translating campaign data into business language the client understood — built the trust that enabled progressive budget increases. Disputes were resolved proactively through data transparency.
Growth Outcome
Account budget grew 67% over two quarters. Conversion volume increased 24% while total cost dropped 16% via efficiency gains from smarter audience targeting — the compounding effect of long-term account management done right.
How I Work
Advisory
Process
A repeatable, client-centred methodology for launching and scaling Google Ads campaigns that consistently deliver commercial results.
1
Discovery & Business Goal Alignment
Every engagement begins with understanding the client's core business — their customer, their margins, their seasonality, and their definition of success. This shapes every campaign decision that follows.
2
Account Audit & Opportunity Analysis
For existing accounts: a structured audit of keyword architecture, bidding strategy, ad copy, and conversion tracking. For new accounts: competitive research and campaign blueprint creation aligned to budget reality.
3
Strategic Campaign Build & Launch
Implements campaigns with clear ad group structure, high-quality ad copy, appropriate bid strategies (Target CPA, Target ROAS, Maximize Conversions), and tracking infrastructure — ensuring the first 30 days generate learning data immediately.
4
Optimization & Performance Review
Monthly optimization cycles include search term analysis, negative keyword additions, bid adjustments, ad creative testing, and budget reallocation toward what the data confirms is working.
5
Scale & Account Expansion
Once the core campaign performs consistently, identifies adjacent growth opportunities — new campaign types, new geographies, new audience segments — and presents data-backed proposals for sustainable account expansion.
